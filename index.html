Escape to Linux

How to Install Linux: A definitive step-by-step guide to making your system faster, cleaner, and fully under your control.

Mission Steps

Get Required Files

1

Locate the official website for your chosen Linux distribution. Download the ISO file specific to your processor architecture.

Download Utility

2

Download a utility to create a bootable USB flash drive, such as Rufus tool setup.

Prepare USB Drive

3

Plug a USB flash drive into the computer (an 8GB stick is recommended). Open Rufus and select the correct flash drive from the "Device" menu.

Configure & Flash

4

Click "Select" to choose your downloaded Linux ISO. Set the partition scheme to GPT for UEFI systems, or MBR for BIOS systems. Click "Start" until status is Ready.

Boot From USB

5

Restart your PC. Tap the boot menu key rapidly as the system powers back on (often F8, F11, or F12). Choose the USB drive using Arrow keys and press Enter.

Install OS

6

Wait for the Linux live environment or setup screen to load. Follow the installer guide and select your target drive when prompted for destination.
